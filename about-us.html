We help you keep track of your team

SAMS solution is designed to automatically store your data in the cloud and syncs across your devices, making it easy to manage workforce on-the-go.

Our Story

In 2015, we became bored of the conventional system of tracking staff proof of attendance and absenteeism because such system was incapable of providing businesses with real-time insight into staff absenteeism, late arrival, early departure, and the time taken on break.

To enable our HR department to operate in compliance with the labour regulations regarding proof of attendance, we decided to build smart attendance management solutions (SAMS) to streamline staff proof of attendance and absenteeism on-the-go while synchronizing data across multiple devices including iOS and Android.

We built and piloted SAMS for 2 years to thoroughly test SAMS effectiveness in maximising staff productivity and business profitability. Consequently, SAMS was scaled up and SAMS Ltd was started in mid-2017. Today, we support over XXX businesses in XXX countries all over the world and still counting.
